About Our Business

At JDS Lighting Repair, we specialize in the meticulous restoration and repair of antique and vintage lighting. With a deep appreciation for craftsmanship and history, we bring new life to chandeliers, lamps, sconces, and other unique fixtures, preserving their beauty while ensuring they meet modern safety standards. Each project is approached with care, using period-appropriate materials and techniques whenever possible, so the charm and character of your treasured piece remain intact. Proudly veteran-owned and serving the Palm Beaches since 2019, we take pride in combining skilled craftsmanship with a passion for keeping the glow of the past alive.
