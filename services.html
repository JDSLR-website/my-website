What We Offer

At JDS Lighting Repair, we provide expert repair, restoration, and fabrication services for antique and vintage lighting fixtures. Whether you have a cherished family heirloom or a unique market find, our skilled craftsmanship ensures your lighting is both beautiful and safe.

Antique Lighting Restoration

We carefully restore vintage and antique lighting fixtures, preserving their original character while updating them to meet modern safety standards.

Custom Fabrication

From replacement parts to custom-designed fixtures, we create high-quality components to match the style and craftsmanship of your existing lighting.

Electrical Rewiring

We rewire antique fixtures using modern UL-listed wiring and components, ensuring your lights are safe and functional without compromising their look.
